The Proposal: A 30-Day YouTube Pilot

Salt City Video Productions proposes to personally lead a focused, low-risk, 30-day pilot on a temporary YouTube channel. This channel will operate as its own self-contained unit, directly showcasing your existing Buzztime programming. This unique approach allows us to collect precise data on digital audience engagement and test the viability of a standalone online presence, providing clear insights for your future strategy.

  • 🎯
    Gauge Audience Interest: Determine the real-world appetite for Buzztime content in a live stream format.
  • 🧪
    Test Content Formats: Experiment with different games and presentation styles to see what resonates most.
  • ⚙️
    Assess Technical Feasibility: Understand the practicalities and challenges of streaming your content live.
  • 📊
    Collect Engagement Data: Analyze viewer numbers, chat activity, and interaction to inform a full-scale strategy.
  • 🔄
    Direct Program Push: Your existing Buzztime programming, optimized for digital display, will be directly streamed onto the temporary YouTube channel.
  • 🏢
    Channel as a Team: The YouTube channel will operate as its own self-contained unit, allowing for precise tracking of its independent performance and audience dynamics.
  • 🔍
    Data-First Approach: Every stream will be a data-gathering opportunity, providing granular insights into what works and what doesn't for an online-first Buzztime experience.
  • 📈
    Viewer Engagement: Track peak concurrent viewers and average watch time.
  • 💬
    Chat Activity: Measure interaction volume and general sentiment.
  • ➕
    Channel Growth: Monitor the rate of new subscribers to the pilot channel.
  • ⭐
    Content Performance: Identify which game types and stream formats generate the most engagement.
